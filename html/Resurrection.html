The Resurrection

These are depictions of Christ's resurrection that have impacted me:



Genevieve Arthy: "Woman at the Tomb"

I liked the cool technique that was used in this painting. It does not matter that I cannot see the faces of Christ or Mary, because the expression of joy, relief and shock of seeing the resurrected Christ is expressed in Mary holding her head in her hands.





Liz Lemon Swindle: "Hope"

Not necessarily a picture of Jesus Christ's resurrection, but I think this is a cool depiction of what it would have been like for these apostles running to see the Savior's empty tomb.





Nathan Greene: "The Resurrection"

This is probably my favorite picture out of all of the ones that I found. I think it is an epic portrayal of Christ leaving the tomb and the guards incapasitated. I had not seen a depiction of Christ's resurrection like this, and I thought it was very unique.
